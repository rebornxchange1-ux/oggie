The Legend

Who Is Oggie

Born in underground comics in 1964, OGGIE inspired the frog culture that later evolved into Pepe and today's meme coin movement. Long before the internet existed, this character was already living rent-free in the imagination of a generation that understood something the mainstream did not.

Long before Dawn, OGGIE embodied the carefree, rebellious spirit of Life Is Good — a mindset that continues to resonate across internet culture, meme communities, and decentralized finance. Some things are bigger than trends.

Today, OGGIE leaps into the BSC pond, reconnecting the roots of comic history with the future of decentralized communities. The legend does not begin here. It continues.

The Journey

Roadmap

Phase 01Foundation
Everything starts with the community. The goal in this phase is to build a strong, engaged base of 1,000 members across Twitter and Telegram. Daily raids and community engagement campaigns will keep the energy alive, while meme contests and community rewards give holders a reason to stay and participate. Alongside this, we will submit coin listing applications and sharpen the branding and visual identity of the project.
Phase 02Growth
With the foundation set, the focus shifts to reaching 1,000 or more holders. Influencer partnerships and collaborations will extend our reach into new audiences, while community AMAs and interactive events deepen trust and engagement. Trending campaigns on X will push visibility, and a wider presence across the meme coin ecosystem will put OGGIE in front of the people who matter.
Phase 03Expansion
This phase is about building real alliances. Strategic partnerships with other meme communities will bring new audiences and energy into the OGGIE ecosystem. Community governance initiatives will give holders a real voice in the direction of the project, and an expanded social media presence will ensure that OGGIE is showing up everywhere it needs to be.
Phase 04Global Recognition
OGGIE goes global. A full-scale marketing campaign will push the project into international conversations. Charity and social impact initiatives will show that this community stands for something beyond speculation. Long-term ecosystem sustainability will be the guiding principle as we build something that lasts well past the hype cycle.
Phase 05Utility and Listings
The final phase unlocks real utility for OGGIE holders. A staking portal will reward long-term believers, and a gaming hub will give the community a new way to earn and engage. On the exchange front, we are targeting listings on major CEX platforms including MEXC, Bitget, OKX, and Binance, putting $OGGIE in front of millions of traders worldwide.
